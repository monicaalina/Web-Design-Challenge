Summary: Latitude vs. Humidity(%)

As shown on the graph, the humidity percentage tends to be higher once the latitude of the cities are getting closer to the equator, and it has a cluster of near the equator.

There is stronger relationship between humidity (%) and latitude in northern hemisphere when compares with the data in southern hemisphere. In northern hemisphere, the humidity percentage increases as the latitude increases as well (the trend is quite obvious and clear). Nevertheless, such trend becomes less obvious in southern hemisphere

This can be a good start point to conduct further research to explore the spread of seasonal respiratory viruses since a warm and humid environment is perfect for the growth of viruses.
